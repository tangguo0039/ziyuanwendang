  • Q3: Centralized wallet? Decentralized wallet? Why can't the central wallet query the transfer?
    For example, your wallet address is A. You transfer address E to 10 USDDT, but you use the transaction hash value to query, and you can only query the transfer of a random ledger address B (or C or D, etc.) to E by the service provider. Similarly, E can't know whether you transferred these 10 USDT, because E can only see that the account book address B transferred 10 USDT to him. This is why the central wallet cannot query the real transfer information on the blockchain.
    Click to view the difference between blockchain wallets
  • Q4: Why do I win or not in a row?
    First of all, it is low-probability events（definition of low-probability events），that do not win many times in a row. Secondly, winning and not winning are random events, and the frequency of random events tends to be stable. In non-iterative situations, the probability of things appearing is equal to everyone, and in this case, everyone is fair. At the same time, random events will follow the law of large numbers (the test is repeated many times under the condition that the test remains unchanged, and the frequency of random events is similar to the definition of its probability large number law). In the case of increasing transfer frequency, the probability of winning and not winning the lottery tends to a stable value. Therefore, please continue to transfer money. As the frequency of transfer increases, the total probability of winning the lottery will increase.

    Because blockchain technology will conduct multiple accounting, comparison and confirmation in order to ensure the security of funds. Therefore, the calculation speed is slow, but the most important thing is safety.
